FinTech API hackathons

14 July 2014
Berlin
SIGN UP

Europe's notorious FinTech Hackathon series "Hack/Make The Bank" comes to Berlin this summer! The 4th edition is an OK Festival fringe event and will have an open data flavour. Developers, designers and members of the financial services industry will gather for a day of brainstorming and software creation in the fields of retail banking, capital markets, payments...etc Attendees will have a host of application programming interfaces (APIs) and open data sets they can leverage and combine in order to bring their innovative ideas to life. They can participate alone or join a team. Participants will demo their hacks at the end of the day to a panel of bank executives, FinTech entrepreneurs and the general public.

Note: Please check back nearer the time, since the venue might change!

Organised by TESOBE / The Open Bank Project
