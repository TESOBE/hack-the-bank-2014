"Hack/Make The Bank" comes to Berlin this summer!

The 4th edition is an OK Festival fringe event with an Open Data flavour.

Developers, designers and members of the financial services industry will gather for a day of brainstorming and software creation in the fields of retail banking, capital markets, payments etc.

Come and hack on the following APIs:

  • Open Bank Project
  • Open Corporates
  • World Bank
  • Enigma.io
  • Spend Network

Organised by TESOBE / The Open Bank Project
